Cricket Batsmen Standard Deviation

The table on this page lists the top 200 best male test cricket batsmen based on total number of runs scored. They've been analysed according to a version of the script you can find in this notebook which was made to accompany this blog post on the standard deviation of cricket averages. As well as coming up with standard deviations, I also come up with two extra, slightly O.T.T. cricketing metrics, which are explained below.

What the new terms mean

$$reliability = {\text{average} \over \text{standard deviation}}$$ $$dependableness = \text{reliability} \times \text{average} = {\text{average}^2 \over \text{standard deviation}}$$
